Facebook Friends

Login to Facebook to send a wapo to any of your Friends. Buy the maximum number of Wapos and any of your friends can download the wapos up to the maximum. Anyone who clicks on link must be validated (they must log in through Facebook) and validated as your friend.

Facebook Pages

Login to Facebook to send a wapo to page followers. Buy a maximum number of Wapos you would like to send and anyone who has liked your page can download the wapo. Anyone who clicks on link must be validated (they must log in through Facebook) and validated to like your page.

Twitter Followers

Login through Twitter and post a link for a Wapo. Buy the maximum number of Wapos for any of your followers to download. Only your followers (Authenticated and confirmed) can download the Wapo.

Selected Twitter Followers

Login through Twitter and post a link for a Wapo. Only the selected followers (Authenticated and confirmed) can download the Wapo.
